Ripples of Wisdom

9 Prefrontal Functions

  1. Bodily regulation. 2 branches: sympathetic (accelerator), and parasympathetic (brakes) .

  2. Attuned communication. "Feeling it".

  3. Emotional balance. The brains basis of equanimity, the ability to stay clear and focused in the face of storms from both inside and outside of us.

  4. Response flexability. Harnesses the power of the middle prefrontal region to put a temporal space between input and action.

  5. Fear modulation.

  6. Empathy. Capacity to create mindsight images of other peoples minds (you-maps.

  7. Insight. Allows us to make me-maps enabling us to perceive our own minds. "Mental time travel", permitting us to experience ourselves as a center of subjective gravity, the author of our own unfolding life story.

  8. Moral awareness. Denotes the ways in which we both think about and enact behaviours for the social good.

  9. Intuition. How the middle prefrontal cortex gives us access to the wisdom of the body. Recieves information from throughout the interior of the body, including the viscera- such as our heart and our intestines- and uses this input to give us a "heart felt sense" of what to do or a "gut feeling" about the right choice.
