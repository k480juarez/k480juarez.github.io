About Mami Vicky and Papi Felipe

Mami and Papi Juarez are well known in their community, among a great deal of things, for the greatness of their hearts and of course...their famous cooking. On any given day if you were to be at "Mami Vicky and Papi Felipe's house", you would hear the sound of Spanish worship music under the sound of family chatter, enveloped in the amazing smell of Papi Felipe roasting tomatoes, grilling chicken and beef, and frying fish and potatoes in the backyard. Walk inside the kitchen and be greated by the delicious aroma of Mami Vicky's homemade corn tamales and atole. When you add to that the kindness of generations of hard working and humble upbringings, it is sure to give you a beautiful sense of affirmed existence that will ripple through time like a fresh breeze on a warm summer day.
